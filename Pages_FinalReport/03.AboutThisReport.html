What is the LGBF?

The LGBF is a benchmarking instrument with a primary objective of empowering senior management teams and elected officials to scrutinise council services. Rooted in the collaborative efforts of SOLACE (Scotland) and COSLA it uses measurement standards and comparable data as catalysts for improving services.

The framework is a key component of each council's legal obligations for public performance reporting. It supersedes the preceding Statutory Performance Indicators (SPI) regimen, reflecting an evolved approach to measuring and enhancing council services.

Key Points

  1. This report presents indicators grouped in an order that reflects the citizen's journey, creating a more meaningful experience both in terms of analytical insights and readability. The indicators are arranged starting from: early education, care, environment, economy, property & regulation, and corporate.
  2. The LGBF Board has approved the inclusion of new indicators to enhance the benchmarking suite. The newly included indicators are:
    • CLIM 03: CO2 emissions from Transport per capita
    • CLIM 04: CO2 emissions from Electricity per capita
    • CLIM 05: CO2 emissions from Natural Gas per capita
